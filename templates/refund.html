Return & Refund Policy

Welcome to 2MB Computer

At 2MB Computer, we want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you may request a return or refund according to the policy below.

1. Return Eligibility

  • Product must be returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • The product must be unused and in original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required.

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Products damaged due to misuse.
  • Items without original packaging.

3. Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the returned product, we will notify you about the approval of your refund.

4. Refund Time

If approved, the refund will be processed within 5-7 business days through the original payment method.

5. Contact Support

If you have any questions about returns or refunds, contact us. Click the link below to send us an email directly.

Email: support@2mbcomputer.com
